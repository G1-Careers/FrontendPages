Deletion Process

Circumstances for Deletion

  • User Request: Users can request account deletion through our support team.
    • 📧 Email Request: Send email to abhishek@g1careers.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request".
  • Policy Violations: Accounts may be deleted for violations of terms and conditions

Deletion Process

  • Users will be notified via email once their account has been deleted.

Deletion Confirmation

  • Users can confirm deletion by trying to re-register with the same email post-deletion.
