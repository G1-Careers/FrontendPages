Accelerate Your Career With G1Careers

Exciting Opportunity: We are building a new team of 50 Java Full Stack Engineers with 3+ years of experience. If you're looking to pivot your career to Java Full Stack, G1Careers will train you and help you land a position with our prestigious clients.

How It Works

If you're a professional with 3+ years of experience and want to transition to Java Full Stack development, this program is designed for you. G1Careers will provide comprehensive training and deployment opportunities at our client locations in Hyderabad and Bangalore.

Application Process

  1. Submit your application
  2. Complete the self-assessment
  3. Take the online technical test
  4. Receive selection notification and offer letter
  5. Pay the security deposit of ₹20,000/-
  6. Undergo 3-6 months of training (duration based on your current skill level)
  7. Participate in 10 interviews with mid-level MNCs in Hyderabad/Bangalore
  8. Receive interview selection results and appointment letter
  9. Sign the service agreement
  10. Begin your new career journey
